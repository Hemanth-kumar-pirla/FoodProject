To Prepare A Tasty And Healthy Veg Biriyani

       INGREDIANTS;                         QUANTITY


       OIL                                                             To Enough


       Basmati rice,soaked                                  1 1/2 cups


       Onions,dice                                                2 medium


       Carrots,1/2 inch pieces                             2 medium


       Cardamoms                                              6(minimum) for more 9 enough


       

       Ghee                                                          To Taste


       Fresh Tomato Paste                                 1 Cup


       Ginger-Garlic- Paste                              2 Tea Spoon


       Chilli Powder                                          To Taste or 3 or 3 1/2(for normal)


       Turmeric Powder                                  2 Tea Spoon


       Garam masala                                      To taste


;

       Salt                                                         2 Spoons


       Chopped Mint                                     2 Table Spoons


       

PROCEESURE TO COOK


           
  1. Boil rice in four cups of salted boiling water with twogreen cardamoms,five cloves, half inch stick of cinnamon, until three-fourth done.
    2.        
  2. Drainexcess water and set aside Heat a non-stick pan.
    3.        
  3. Add theremaining green cardamoms, cloves, black cardamom andcinnamon along with bay leaf and caraway seeds androast.
    4.        
  4. Add onions, carrot, French beans, cauliflower florets and green peas.
    5.        
  5. Sprinkle salt, cover and cook onmedium heat for two minutes Add ginger-garlic pastemixed with a little water and stir.
    6.        
  6. Cover and cook for twominutes Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder andcoriander powder and cook.
    7.        
  7. Whisk yogurt with rose water and saffron Add a little water or milk and whisk well.
    8.        
  8. Add tomato puree to the vegetables along with half teaspoon garam masala powder and mix well Simmer for two minutes. Take a microwave safe deep bowl.
    9.        
  9. Arrange a layer of rice at the bottom Over that arrange half thecooked vegetables followed by another layer of rice.
    10.        
  10. Sprinkle half of the remaining garam masala powder, half the coriander leaves, half the mint leaves and half theyogurt mixture.
    11.        
  11. Arrange the remaining vegetablesfollowed by the remaining rice Sprinkle the remaininggaram masala powder, remaining coriander leaves,remaining mint leaves and the remaining yogurt mixture.
    12.        
  12. Cover with a silicon lid and cook in the microwave ovenfor four to five minutes on HIGH (100%).
    13.        
  13. Let it stand for five minutes. Serve hot.
